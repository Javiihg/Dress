About Me

I’m Javi, a multidisciplinary freelance designer specializing in 3D visualization and motion design. I create visual solutions that blend technical precision with a refined aesthetic, tailored for brands, creative studios, and independent projects.

My work spans from modeling, shading, and photorealistic rendering to animations crafted for product, spatial, and brand storytelling. I also collaborate on fashion concept development in 3D and visual direction.

At the core of my practice is empathy. Knowing that there’s a complex, unique individual on the other side of the screen drives me to adopt a multi-layered approach to design.

My main workflow revolves around Blender and Houdini, which allow me to merge design and motion seamlessly, though I’m adaptable to other 3D design tools as well.
